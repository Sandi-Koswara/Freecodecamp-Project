Portgas D. Ace

The man who saved Monkey D. Luffy

Here's time line of Portgas D. Ace's life in One Piece Manga:

  • Vol. 56 Chapter 550 - Portgas D. Ace was born as Gol D. Ace to Gol D. Roger (Pirate King) and Portgas D. Rouge
  • Vol. 56 Chapter 551 - Portgas D. Rouge carried Ace for 20 months to prevent the World Government from knowing about his birth and connection to Gol D. Roger
  • Vol. 59 Chapter 585 & 589 - Ace's early childhood was spent in the care of the noble family in the Goa Kingdom
  • Vol. 59 Chapter 585-588 - Ace meets Monkey D. Luffy and Sabo in his childhood and forms a brotherhood with them
  • Vol. 15 Chapter 133-153 - Ace sets out to sea and becomes a pirate to pursue his dream of becoming the Pirate King
  • Vol. 45 Chapter 434 - Ace becomes a member of the Whitebeard Pirates and rises to the position of the 2nd division commander
  • Vol. 46 Chapter 440 & 441 - Ace encounters Blackbeard (Marshall D. Teach) and is defeated by him
  • Vol. 51 Chapter 501 - Ace is captured by the Marines and held in Impel Down
  • Vol. 56 Chapter 551 - The "Summit War of Marineford" takes place as the Whitebeard Pirates and their allies try to rescue Ace
  • Vol. 53 Chapter 516 - Ace is sentenced to death by execution at Marineford
  • Vol. 59 Chapter 573 - Ace sacrifices himself to save Luffy from Admiral Akainu, leading to his tragic death
  • Vol. 60 Chapter 581 - Ace's death has a profound impact on Luffy and the entire One Piece world

"Even though Ace is gone, his spirit lives on in me. I will take care and make our dreams come true together."

-- Straw Hat Pirates Captain Monkey D. Luffy
